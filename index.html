About the Senior Library

The Senior Library at Gyanshree School is a state-of-the-art resource center designed to support the academic and intellectual growth of students from grades VI to XII.

With a vast collection of fiction, non-fiction, reference materials, and digital databases, the library serves as a quiet sanctuary for study and research.
